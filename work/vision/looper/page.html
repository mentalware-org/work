Time loop

Layers rolled in time

The loop of mentalware cycle is rolling around mental work and consists of the following stages:

  • Design. The main thinking stage. Mental work started from it and each loop contains it. Thinker fixes the core concepts and hypothesis and deducts all predictable consequences. In each moment of time all layers are changed together with no or small relation to mentalware timing. The most of changes are going from concepts to content through methods.
  • Observation. The main empiric stage for test, research and other experience. Here the mentalware time comes to direct contact with real time of thinker and vice versa. Stage is finished with evaluation of results.
  • Collection. All recorded content, methods and facts are collected, organized and prepared for publication. Here the time
